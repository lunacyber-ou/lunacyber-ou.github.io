Services

OUR SERVICES

Penetration Testing

We offer comprehensive penetration testing services to identify vulnerabilities in your systems, applications, and networks. Our expert team simulates real-world cyberattacks to ensure your digital assets are secure.

Red Team

Our Red Team experts specialize in advanced threat simulations to assess your organization’s readiness against sophisticated adversaries. We employ cutting-edge tactics to uncover weaknesses and strengthen your defenses.

Source Code Analysis

Luna Cyber provides in-depth source code analysis to ensure the security and integrity of your software applications. We help you identify and eliminate code-level vulnerabilities to protect your data and reputation.

Consultancy

Our cybersecurity consultancy services are tailored to your specific needs. We provide expert guidance, risk assessments, and strategic recommendations to enhance your overall security posture.

Training

We offer customized cybersecurity training programs to empower your team with the knowledge and skills needed to defend against cyber threats. Stay one step ahead with Luna Cyber’s training solutions.

Digital Forensic

In the event of a security incident, our digital forensic experts are here to investigate and uncover the facts. We employ the latest forensic techniques to gather evidence and support incident response efforts.
